About

Hi, I'm Robert Cooper, a highly motivated person that has an eye for detail and a passion for technology. I graduated from Queen's University with a Bachelor of Science in Mechanical Engineering in April of 2017. During my time at university I enjoyed to the programming aspect involved in my courses and started to look into opportunities involved with web development.

In the summer of 2016, I ran my own web design business where I designed websites for small business owners using WordPress and Shopify. In the fall of 2016, I shutdown the business to finish my final year of studies. During that last year of University I became involved in as many web development opportunities as I could. Between the fall of 2016 up until my graduation in April of 2017, I took on the role of Director of Technology for a technology conference, worked part-time building websites for a digital marketing agency, and was involved as a member of the Engineering Society IT team where we built an online student forum.
